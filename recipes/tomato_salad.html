Ingredients

  • 4 Large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thymed leaves
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh Oregano
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlick
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange the tomato slices in a shallow casserole dish. Whisk together the olive oil, thyme, oregano, parsley, chives, and garlic in a small bowl. Add salt and pepper to taste. Pour herb mixture over the tomatoes, overing evenly. Top with Parmesan cheese. Cover, and refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
