Ingredients

  • 1 package lasana noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 3/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles in a large pot of water for 10 minutes
  2. Heat Oil in a large saucepan. Add mushrooms, green peppers, onion, and carlick; cook and stir until tender
  3. While the sauce is simmering, mix together ricotta, 2 cups mozzarella, and eggs in a medium bowl
  4. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees. Grease a baking dish.
  5. Spread 1 cup cooked tomato and vegetable sauce into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Lay down 1/2 of the lasagna noodles and layer 1/2 each of the ricotta mix, sauce, and Parmesan cheese. Top with remainig 2 cups mozzarella.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven, uncovered, for 40 minutes. Let for 15 minutes before serving.
